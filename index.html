Sri Lanka Blended Experience

10 days · 9 nights · Cultural + Wildlife + Beach

Duration 10 Days / 9 Nights
Group Size Up to 4 (bus on request)
Distance ~150 km/day
Best For First-time visitors
Arrive at Colombo Airport. Transfer to a hotel of your choice in Negombo or Colombo. Relax and recover from your flight.
Travel to the cultural triangle with an en-route visit to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. In the afternoon, enjoy a jeep safari at Minneriya National Park to spot wild elephants. Overnight in Dambulla or Sigiriya.
Explore the ancient kingdom of Anuradhapura, including the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi tree, Ruwanwelisaya pagoda, and other ancient ruins. Return to Dambulla/Sigiriya for the night.
Visit Dambulla Cave Temple en route. Stop at a spice garden in Matale. Arrive in Kandy and visit the Temple of the Tooth. Overnight in Kandy.
Travel to Nuwara Eliya. Visit Ramboda Waterfall and a Damro tea factory. Spend a relaxing afternoon walking around the hill town. Overnight in Nuwara Eliya.
Early morning visit to Horton Plains (5:00 AM start). Visit Eagle View Point. Enjoy Victoria Park and Gregory Lake. Take the scenic Odyssey train from Nuwara Eliya to Ella. Overnight in Ella.
Sunrise hike to Ella Rock (4 hours). Visit the Nine Arch Bridge and Little Adam's Peak. Travel to Yala with a stop at Ravana Falls. Overnight in Tissamaharama or Yala.
Drive to Mirissa for whale watching. Visit the Dutch Fort in Galle. Enjoy the Madu River boat safari and visit a Turtle Hatchery. Stop at Bentota. Overnight in Bentota.
Drive to Colombo. Visit Gangaramaya Temple, Lotus Tower, Port City, the Colombo Museum, and One Galle Face Mall. Overnight in Colombo or Negombo.
After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your departure. I'll make sure you're at the gate with time to spare.
